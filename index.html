Tulsi

Tulsi or Holy basil is a widely known herb in the family Lamiaceae. It is native to India and vastly cultivated throughout Southeast Asia.

Ginger

Known for its warming properties and is often used to support digestion and reduce inflammation

Turmeric

Known for its anti-inflammatory properties and is often used to support digestion and liver function.

Ashwagandha

Known as Indian ginseng, this herb is believed to promote balance in the body, mind and spirit, and is often used to reduce stress, improve sleep and support the immune system.

Neem

Known for its purifying properties and is often used to support the skin and liver function.

Licorice root(Yashtimadhu)

Believed to have a nourishing and grounding effect on the body and is often used to support the adrenal glands and reduce stress
